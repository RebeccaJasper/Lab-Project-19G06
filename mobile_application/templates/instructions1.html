Identi.Fi App

Instructions

You're about to be shown a picture of a non-existent GAN person for 15 seconds. Study the person's face as closely as possible - their face shape, eye shape, mouth shape, eyebrow shape and nose shape - in the alloted time.

After the 15 seconds are over, you're going to be asked to fill out a short form about the person that you just saw. Please answer the questions as accurately as possible.

After you fill in the form, you're going to be directed to the Identi-Kreate tool, where you'll get a chance to try and recreate the person's face. Please try and recreate the person's face as accurately as possible.

Once you're done with that, and have pressed the "Submit" button, you're done!

When you're ready to view the image, press the "Start" button below.
