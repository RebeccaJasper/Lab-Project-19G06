Identi.Fi App

The Big Idea

In real life, it's often hard for authorities to find a person from peoples' descriptions alone.

The Identi.Fi App aims to change that.

The app aims to allow you to give a brief description of a person and create a facial composite, a.k.a an identikit, of the person in an easy, user-friendly way. With this description, our Identi.Match App will allow for authorities to find possible matches to your description in an existing person database.

The potential, positive impact that this app can have is huge. Some possible uses for it include:

  • Finding missing persons
  • Identifying suspects
  • Etc.

Please help us make this all possible by helping identify an "unknown" person that we're about to introduce to you. Your participation will really help us make this app as effective as possible.
