Identi.Fi App

Instructions

The control panel shown on the right is the same control panel that you will be using when you recreate the unknown person's face. It is therefore advised that you examine these characteristics on the person you will see carefully.

Please note that this is a Beta version of the app and so there are a few glitches when displaying the app. If the Identikit stretches on window resize, just click on one of the sliders to make it look normal again. If you would like to zoom; scroll and then click on one of the sliders to see the effects of the zoom. If this is confusing you - ignore it. These shouldn't be major problems that hinder your ability to use the app.

When you're ready to view the image, press the "Start" button below.
